Where does the Web come from?

The Social Web – Web 2.0

Web 2.0 marks the shift to the interactive and participatory Web.
It enabled users not only to consume content but also to create, share, and collaborate online.
Most websites served as online brochures or repositories of information, where users could view content but could not easily contribute or modify it.
The Web became a space of collaboration and user-generated content, often referred to as the Social Web.
